The woman called Faith who never gave up on her dreams

The woman of faith called Faith

I met a girl on facebook called Faith,
Although she's inquisitive but her moral was faint.
She had gone through a lot and had little faith,
but she was open minded to learn and receive like a wide plate.

She was ready to rise and started getting courage,
she worked during the day and read at night without being discouraged.
Although her best food ain't portage,
but after running to Christ for renewal her life entered a new page.

Then she became one of the greatest,
she wrote many exams and came out the best.
because of her great faith she was always seperated from the rest,
She became a testimony, the woman who broke herself out of every nest.
